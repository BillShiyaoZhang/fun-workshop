About this workshop

关于本坊

This is for fun! Share your interesting projects with others. Meet new friends who share your interests. Learn new skills and have a great time!

Fun Workshop aims to provide sincere information and insights, so we plan to show full respect to the speakers. We expect speakers to inform us of the materials they will use before the event, but we do not restrict the speakers' self-determination for any factors other than legal awareness.

We welcome participants from all backgrounds and skill levels. Currently, the topics collected cover multiple fields, including AI, business, interaction design, personal growth, non-mainstream perspectives, etc. We cherish the goodwill and courage of all speakers.

Therefore, to maintain the community atmosphere, we sincerely suggest that everyone do not spread the information discussed in the community, and less judge the speaker's expression. Currently, we only promote activities on the Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University campus.

The workshop will be held at XJTLU, Suzhou, China from March to May, 2026. Stay tuned for more details about the schedule in the Program page.

Fun Workshop supports a bilingual environment in Chinese and English. In environments that natively support language translation (e.g., WeChat), the information we publish uses the language spoken by the majority; in environments without native translation support, we provide bilingual materials (e.g., posters) or real-time language translation services (e.g., using the built-in translation function of Tencent Meeting at the event).

For online meetings, it should be noted that Fun Workshop does not guarantee that remote online participation is provided for all activities, due to various considerations including topic sensitivity.

Fun Workshop plans to collect, store and edit audio and video data of speakers at the event site after obtaining consent, and use it for appropriate dissemination and sharing after explicit confirmation. However, we do not collect any other data before explicit notification and explicit authorization.

Fun Workshop voluntarily adopts the academic ethics standards of Xi'an Jiaotong-Liverpool University, publishes the corresponding documents, and welcomes everyone's supervision.

Fun Workshop has set up a website for long-term information sharing. All kinds of information will be posted simultaneously in the WeChat group and on the website. Website address: https://billshiyaozhang.github.io/fun-workshop/

Currently, Fun Workshop is still running on a volunteer basis. While we encourage everyone to join the operation team, we also remind you to be cautious.

For any questions, please contact the coordinator Shiyao Zhang at shiyao.zhang14@student.xjtlu.edu.cn.

Fun Workshop 旨在提供诚恳的信息与见解，因此我们计划为分享者表示充分的尊重。我们期待分享者在活动前知会其将使用的材料，但我们不因除法律自觉外的其他因素限制分享者的自决。

我们欢迎所有背景和技能水平的参与者。 目前，已征集到的分享话题囊括多个领域，AI、商业、交互设计、个人成长、非主流视角等。我们珍视各位分享者的善意与勇气。

因此，为了维护社区氛围，我们谨建议各位不传播社区内讨论信息，少评判分享者表达。目前我们仅面向西交利物浦大学校园进行活动宣传。

本工作坊将于 2026 年 3 月至 5 月在中国苏州的西交利物浦大学举行。 请关注日程（Program）页面以获取更多详细信息。

Fun Workshop 支持中英双语环境。在原生支持语言翻译的环境（如，微信），我们发布的信息采用使用人数多的语言；在非原生支持语言翻译的环境，我们提供双语材料（如，海报），或实时语言翻译服务（如，在活动现场利用腾讯会议内置翻译功能）。

对于线上会议，需要说明的是，Fun Workshop 并不保证所有活动都提供远程线上参与活动，这是出于包括话题敏感度在内的多种考量。

Fun Workshop 有计划在争得同意后采集、存储并编辑活动现场分享者的音视频数据，并经过明确确认后用于适度的传播分享。但我们不在未进行明确告知并获得明确授权前采集任何其他数据。

Fun Workshop 自愿采用西交利物浦大学学术伦理标准，公示相应文件，并欢迎各位监督。

Fun Workshop 已经搭建了网站用于长期信息共享。各类信息均将同步在微信群与网站发布。网站地址：https://billshiyaozhang.github.io/fun-workshop/

目前 Fun Workshop 仍是志愿运行。在我们鼓励各位加入运行团队的同时，也提醒慎重。

如有任何问题，请联系协调人 Shiyao Zhang：shiyao.zhang14@student.xjtlu.edu.cn。
