About this workshop

This is for fun! Share your interesting projects with others. Meet new friends who share your interests. Learn new skills and have a great time!

The workshop will be held at XJTLU, Suzhou, China from March to May, 2026. Stay tuned for more details about the schedule in the Program page.

We welcome participants from all backgrounds and skill levels. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced practitioner, there is something for everyone at this workshop. Join us for a fun and engaging experience!

For any questions, please contact the coordinator Shiyao Zhang at shiyao.zhang14@student.xjtlu.edu.cn.
